        <h1>Giuliani: Hunter Biden&#8217;s Attorney Contacted Computer Repairman After NY Post Story Broke, Wanted Hard Drive Back</h1>
        <div class="flex items-center mb-5 text-sm italic font-normal font-title md:text-xl md:mb-10">
  <div class="flex-1 text-right">
    By <a href="https://amgreatness.com/author/debra-heine/" rel="author" class="fn meta-link">
      Debra Heine
    </a>
  </div>
  <div class="flex-none mx-4">
        <img class="inline-block w-20 h-20 text-white bg-gray-100 author-avatar shadow-solid" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E" alt="" data-lazy-src="https://overland.amgreatness.com/app/themes/american-greatness-theme/dist/images/ag-mark_90833ec2.svg" /><noscript><img class="inline-block w-20 h-20 text-white bg-gray-100 author-avatar shadow-solid" src="https://overland.amgreatness.com/app/themes/american-greatness-theme/dist/images/ag-mark_90833ec2.svg" alt="" /></noscript>
        <!-- img class="inline-block w-20 h-20 text-white author-avatar shadow-solid" src="" alt="American Greatness Author" -->
    <!-- img class="inline-block w-20 h-20 -ml-2 text-white author-avatar shadow-solid" src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1550525811-e5869dd03032?ixlib=rb-1.2.1&auto=format&fit=facearea&facepad=2&w=256&h=256&q=80" alt="" -->

    <time class="updated" datetime="2020-10-16T15:21:57+00:00">October 16, 2020</time>

  <div class="entry-content relative  dropcap ">
    <p>After <em>the New York Post</em> broke the bombshell report detailing evidence found on a computer hard drive of the Biden family&#8217;s influence peddling, Hunter Biden&#8217;s attorney contacted the computer repairman who found the incriminating material, and asked for the computer hard drive back, President Trump&#8217;s personal lawyer Rudy Giuliani revealed on Steven Crowder&#8217;s podcast, Thursday.</p>
<p>In addition to that, the former New York mayor divulged that there are dozens of &#8220;disturbing&#8221; photos of Hunter Biden &#8220;doing an imitation of Anthony Weiner,&#8221; and many more indicating that he had &#8220;a very massive drug addition.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;He&#8217;s like a zombie,&#8221; Giuliani told Crowder.</p>
<p>He also said there are texts describing a transaction between Hunter and Joe Biden and &#8220;a Communist Chinese intelligence operative&#8221;—which appeared to have been a set up for extortion.</p>
<p>The former New York mayor summed up <a href="https://nypost.com/2020/10/14/email-reveals-how-hunter-biden-introduced-ukrainian-biz-man-to-dad/"><em>the NY Post</em> bombshell</a> for Crowder:  &#8220;Hunter Biden and Joe Biden are involved in a bribery scheme in Ukraine and Hunter Biden and Joe for years have said that Joe knew nothing about Burisma, the company that paid the bribe,&#8221; he explained.</p>
<p>&#8220;And what there is, is a cold, clear group of text messages in which Hunter Biden sets up a meeting with the number two person in that company and Joe Biden, who then texts Hunter back and thanks him. So it makes Joe a complete liar for the last seven years. It show that he met with Burisma,&#8221; Giuliani said.</p>
<p>The former mayor told Crowder that another message shows that Hunter was snuck into the State Department for a meeting with the then-Deputy Sec. of State Tony Blinken and that the meeting was not recorded because it &#8220;violated the Foreign Agents Registration Act&#8221; (FARA).</p>
<p>When asked how he could prove that the damning texts and emails came from Hunter Biden&#8217;s computer, Giuliani revealed that Biden&#8217;s lawyer contacted John Paul Mac Isaac, the Delaware computer repair shop owner who had discovered the material, as soon as the story broke.</p>
<p>&#8220;I&#8217;ll give you something that really verifies it,&#8221; Giuliani said. &#8220;The very morning this came out, Hunter Biden&#8217;s lawyer called the merchant, and asked him if he could have the hard drive back,&#8221; he said. &#8220;We have email evidence of that. So if the lawyer is asking for the hard drive back—<em>it must be the hard drive.&#8221;</em></p>
<p>&#8220;He actually did it by telephone and email and we have the email,&#8221; Giuliani added.</p>
<p>Crowder was stunned.</p>
<p>&#8220;It&#8217;s almost as if it makes it worse on them, Mr. Giuliani!&#8221; he exclaimed.</p>
<p>&#8220;Oh yeah, it&#8217;s the dumbest thing a lawyer can do,&#8221; Giuliani agreed. &#8220;&#8221;I mean, Joe Biden was the dumbest kid in law school.&#8221;</p>
<p>Giuliani told Crowder that the hard drive makes clear that Hunter suffered from a &#8220;very massive drug addiction.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;It&#8217;s hard to get through a line of photographs without seeing one of him with a crack pipe,&#8221; he said. &#8220;I&#8217;m serious—sitting in a closet going bonkers. He&#8217;s like a zombie.&#8221;</p>
<p>When asked if he&#8217;s seen the photos of Hunter Biden and the crack pipe himself, Giuliani replied, &#8220;you&#8217;re darn right I have!&#8221;</p>
<p>He held up his laptop for Crowder to see, and said they were on that hard drive.</p>
<p>Giuliani said that when Isaac first saw the damning emails, &#8220;he almost fainted&#8221; because &#8220;he&#8217;d never seen anything like this before.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;It&#8217;s got him [Hunter Biden] there with crack pipes, it&#8217;s got him there doing an imitation of Anthony Weiner about 50 times,&#8221; the attorney revealed, as Crowder burst out laughing.</p>
<p>&#8220;You know what I&#8217;m talking about,&#8221; Giuliani added.</p>
<p>Anthony Weiner is the disgraced former Democrat congressman from New York who resigned from Congress in 2011 amid <a href="https://time.com/4471006/anthony-weiner-sexting-scandal-huma-abedin/">a sexting scandal</a> that involved underage girls.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/anthony-weiner-sentenced-nearly-two-years-prison-sexting-scandal-n803906">In 2017,</a> Weiner pleaded guilty to separate sexting charge of transferring obscene material to a minor, and was sentenced to 21 months in prison.</p>
<p>Giuliani went on to soberly note that the hard drive has &#8220;some disturbing things on it that I shouldn&#8217;t joke about.&#8221;</p>
<p>When asked what the next shoe to drop would be, the lawyer said it would have to do with a transaction between Hunter Biden and a &#8220;Chinese communist intelligence operative.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;And it&#8217;s quite obvious that he setting him up. He&#8217;s setting Hunter Biden up and he&#8217;s setting Joe up for extortion,&#8221; Giuliani explained. &#8220;I think there will be more emails and text messages that make that clear.&#8221;</p>
<p>He went on to say that there would be more communications that would describe how Joe Biden was being compensated.</p>
<p>&#8220;In fact, he was getting a large portion of this money,&#8221; Giuliani said, adding that the information would explain how Joe Biden, who has never made that much money as a politician, &#8220;has two or three luxurious homes.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;Because he didn&#8217;t pay for anything, Hunter did,&#8221; he explained.</p>
<p>&#8220;This is a long term bribery scheme that started low level in Delaware with his brother James—selling his office,&#8221; Giuliani told Crowder.</p>
<p>&#8220;When they got to the big time, they shook down Iraq for &#8230; I think about 500 million, Ukraine for about 20 [million], China—I don&#8217;t know—30, 40 million?&#8221; he said.</p>
<p>Giuliani added that he almost forgot Russia. &#8220;The 3.5 million from the mayor&#8217;s wife,&#8221; who he noted is a good friend of Russian President Vladimir Putin. &#8220;That woman is a close ally of Putin,&#8221; Giuliani said, pointing out the irony of the president being accused of colluding with Russia, when &#8220;Biden actually got paid by Russia!&#8221;</p>
<p>Crowder noted that the same Facebook and Twitter that is &#8220;throttling&#8221; this story because it is &#8220;unverified,&#8221; was happy to help push the Russia collusion hoax for years, including the ludicrous &#8220;pee tape&#8221; story, that alleged Trump watched prostitutes urinate on furniture in a Russian hotel room.</p>
<p>&#8220;None of that was throttled,&#8221; Crowder pointed out.</p>
<p>&#8220;And he&#8217;s a germaphobe to boot!&#8221; Giuliani exclaimed. &#8220;He wouldn&#8217;t get within ten miles of something like that!&#8221;</p>
