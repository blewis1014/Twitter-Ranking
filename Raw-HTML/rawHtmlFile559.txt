<div id="article-content" class="entry-content">
<div class="vcard"><p class="fn org">H-Metro</p><img class="photo" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/hmetro_logo_small.jpg" /></div>
<p><strong>Latwell Nyangu, H-Metro Reporter</strong></p>
<p>LOCAL football has been given the greenlight to resume action by the Sports Commission, on condition that it will on the bubble concept.<span id="more-82111"></span></p>
<p>The greenlight is for the national teams, the Premier Soccer League and the Women Soccer League, which means Division One clubs will remain sidelined in the meantime.</p>
<p><img class="lazyload wp-image-82113 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/themes/sirius/img/default_medium.jpg" data-src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" data-srcset=" https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /><noscript><img class="wp-image-82113 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" srcset="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A0661-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /></noscript></p><div class="sirius-dfp-inarticle"> <ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block; text-align:center;" data-ad-layout="in-article" data-ad-format="fluid" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3993920789177136" data-ad-slot="2721990121"></ins><script>(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});</script></div>
<p>It also remains to be seen if the PSL will organise a championship under the conditions, as the bubble concept is a costly exercise, especially considering that matches will be before empty stadiums.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>When the South African Premiership and Division One resumed in July to complete the 2019/20 season, the bill for the bubble concept stood at R100 million, at least according to South African Football Association CEO, Tebogo Motlanthe.</p>
<p><img class="lazyload wp-image-82112 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/themes/sirius/img/default_medium.jpg" data-src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" data-srcset=" https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /><noscript><img class="wp-image-82112 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" srcset="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A5139-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /></noscript></p><div class="sirius-dfp-inarticle"> <ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block; text-align:center;" data-ad-layout="in-article" data-ad-format="fluid" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3993920789177136" data-ad-slot="5085742661"></ins><script>(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});</script></div>
<p>Teams were expected to cover their own expenses, another requirement that makes it very difficult in Zimbabwe as most clubs have no solid financial backing and often forego camping and overnight accommodation.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>In a letter to ZIFA, SRC director general, Prince Mupazviriho said:</p>
<p><img class="lazyload wp-image-82117 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/themes/sirius/img/default_medium.jpg" data-src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" data-srcset=" https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /><noscript><img class="wp-image-82117 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" srcset="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/IMG_3160-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /></noscript></p>
<p>“The Sports and Recreation (SRC) hereby gives your notice, following your application, that the Minister of Youth, Sport, Arts and Recreation approved the gradual and phased resumption of football activities effective October 16 in line with the provisions of Statutory instrument 200 of 2020.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p><div class="sirius-dfp-inarticle"> <ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block; text-align:center;" data-ad-layout="in-article" data-ad-format="fluid" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3993920789177136" data-ad-slot="1855631015"></ins><script>(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});</script></div>
<p>“Please note that approval at this initial stage is for the following:</p>
<p><img class="lazyload wp-image-82120 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/themes/sirius/img/default_medium.jpg" data-src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" data-srcset=" https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /><noscript><img class="wp-image-82120 size-full aligncenter" src="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403.jpg" alt="" width="680" height="453" srcset="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403.jpg 680w, https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/10/287A8403-360x240.jpg 360w" sizes="(max-width: 680px) 100vw, 680px" /></noscript></p><div class="sirius-related"><h3>Related Stories:</h3><ul class="sirius-linked-articles"><li>
<a href="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/caf-explain-nss-ban-reprieve/" rel="bookmark" title="CAF explain NSS ban reprieve"><img class="lazyimg" data-lazy="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/03/national-sports-4-1-100x100.jpg" src="" /><div><span class="s-linked-article-title">CAF explain NSS ban reprieve</span><span class="s-meta-date">16 Oct, 2020</span></div></a></li><li>
 <a href="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/murwira-welcomes-delay/" rel="bookmark" title="Murwira welcomes delay"><img class="lazyimg" data-lazy="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2020/01/Godknows-Murwira-100x100.jpg" src="" /><div><span class="s-linked-article-title">Murwira welcomes delay</span><span class="s-meta-date">08 May, 2020</span></div></a></li><li>
<a href="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/psl-clubs-embrace-change/" rel="bookmark" title="PSL CLUBS EMBRACE CHANGE"><img class="lazyimg" data-lazy="https://www.hmetro.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2019/10/CAPS-United-100x100.jpg" src="" /><div><span class="s-linked-article-title">PSL CLUBS EMBRACE CHANGE</span><span class="s-meta-date">06 May, 2020</span></div></a></li></ul><div class="s-slick-nav"><span><i class="s-icon-circle-left"></i></span><span><i class="s-icon-circle-right"></i></span></div></div>
<p>“Premier Soccer Leagues teams, Women soccer league team and national soccer teams.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>“The resumption of football activities shall take the format of the mini-league using the bubble concept adopted by yourselves.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>“The football activities shall in addition to the requirements specified by the World Health Organisation (WHO) for safe resumption of sports, Statutory Instrument 200 of 2020 submitted protocols, other legislation and police directives relating to the prevention and containment of Covid-19, be subject to the following conditions:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p><div class="sirius-dfp-inarticle"> <ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block; text-align:center;" data-ad-layout="in-article" data-ad-format="fluid" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3993920789177136" data-ad-slot="6457877543"></ins><script>(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});</script></div>
<p>“Strict adherence to the bubble concept, implementation of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and ZIFA and its affiliates to provide resources and finances to undertake these activities.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Added the statement:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>“We reiterate that this is a phased approach to resumption of football activities in the country. Assessments will be done during this initial stage on whether or not all provisions are in place and adherence to set protocols in order to inform the complete resumption of all football activities.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p><div class="sirius-dfp-inarticle"> <ins class="adsbygoogle" style="display:block; text-align:center;" data-ad-layout="in-article" data-ad-format="fluid" data-ad-client="ca-pub-3993920789177136" data-ad-slot="1006240312"></ins><script>(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});</script></div>
<p>“Please not that all the protocols for the prevention and containment of Covid-19 must be observed at all times and SRC expect strict and full compliance by all your members.”</p>
