LANCASTER — Lancaster quarterback Glenn Rice Jr. accounted for seven touchdowns — throwing for six and running for one — in a rout of Newman Smith.
Lancaster (2-0, 2-0) has won its first two games by a combined score of 142-6.
Lancaster’s defense forced six turnovers. Newman Smith (2-1, 1-1) managed just five first downs and 75 total yards in the District 6-5A Division I game.
DQ James led Lancaster with 135 yards rushing and a score.
