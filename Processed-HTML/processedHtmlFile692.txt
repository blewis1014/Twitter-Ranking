Japan Online VR ART FAIR 2020
 
10.17-10.31
Japan Online VR Art Fair 2020 utilizes cutting edge VR technology, aiming to provide in a new way art information for collectors around the world. The technology has set the exhibition free from the time and space constraints of conventional art fairs by presenting the gallery space on a virtual platform. This virtual show offers an immersive experience to the collectors in a world that has been struck by COVID-19. The art fair features six up-and-coming contemporary and two long-established antiques galleries.
